Why I have this site?

I love movies, I love TV, I love food, I love Anime. I love nearly every form of media entertainment that has subdued the 21st century with it's powers of distraction. But I don't love it blidnly by just doing and leaving and talking and not listening, I love these mediums of entertainment because they have an artistry to them that I don't find as appealing as the intelligencia of today do. For example while an art collector may appreciate the subtlety of the strokes, and the emotion of the colours in an Artist's painting, I find joy in the passion behind the making of a movie or video game, and the attention to detail that should accompany it. This site is merely just a template for me as I continue building a portfolio of videogame and movie related work, while also attempting to hone my skills in web design to make even better more appealing sites that would eventually lead to a career combbining the too that I know I excell at purely based on the passion and enjoyment I have in both fields.
