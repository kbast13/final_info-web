PC 2017

August 16, 2017

by Gamespot Staff

PC gamers have experienced a new multiplayer phenomenon in Playerunknown's Battlegrounds, which has taken 2017 by storm. A few PC exclusives like Warhammer 40,000: Dawn of War III and Rising Storm 2, and two console classics--Bayonetta and Vanquish--hit the PC this year in all their glory. But while those games have shined in their own respect, plenty of great games may have flown under your radar. In this gallery, we list our favorite games on PC that you might've missed this year.
