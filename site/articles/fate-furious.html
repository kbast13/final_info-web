Fate of The Furious: Still Riding

For long movie franchises that decide to churn out the same template for plot line, character development, gags, personality traits, seeing the title for each chapter is usually met with a roll of eye, scoff of predictability and comment of derision. The Fast and Furious franchise has not been exempt from the “this is getting ridiculous” thinking from the movie watching public. With an interesting start in 2001 one with title piece; the series has had it’s ups and downs, making use of ever advancing technology and allure of featuring props to keep a name of excitement, and intensity. The public was treated to the 8th instalment the fast cars, beautiful people, and extreme action franchise this year; however, despite the loss of a favourite character, this new addition to the Fast family does not let down the standard its predecessors have established. Fate of The Furious successfully proved that sometimes you couldn’t give up.
